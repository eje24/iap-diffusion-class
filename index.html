Generative AI with Stochastic Differential Equations - IAP 2025

Diffusion and flow-based models have become the state of the art for generative modeling tasks across a wide range of modalities, including images, videos, shapes, molecules, music, and more! This course aims to offer a well-motivated theoretical treatment of the mathematical framework underlying these model families. At the end of the class, students will have built a latent diffusion model from scratch, and along the way will have gained hands on experience with a wide-array of mathematical tools including stochastic differential equations, and the continuity and Fokker-Planck equations.

Logistics

The class consists of three components:
  • Lectures will emphasize core concepts and intuitions.
  • Notes will offer more technical elaborations to arguments presented in lectures.
  • Labs will be given as homework and will allow students to implement ideas from the lectures and notes themselves in a guided manner.
